##instructions on  making tea styled as Pseudocode

```
fill kettle;
turn kettle on;
place teabag in cup;
WHILE kettle is not full:
  DO nothing;
ENDWHILE
fill cup 5/6ths full of boiling water;
allow teabag to stew to taste;
remove teabag;
top up with milk to taste;
IF sugar is required:
  THEN add sugar;
ELSE:
  do nithing;
END
```
